The Impossible Safe

June 2021 · iPhone App

A few years ago I saw Rafael Conde's Break This Safe, but when I got to try it for myself it didn't seem to work as intended. Maybe a couple WatchOS releases broke something vital for the game.

However the concept stuck in my head and when I created my first iPhone App, The Box Timer, I tought that "hey, this is probably something I can pull off".

With my newfound confidence I spent a couple of weekends hacking away, trying to build something that was both fun and simple enought for me to actually complete.

The result is the game you see today – enjoy it as much as I did creating it!
