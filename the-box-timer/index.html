The Box Timer – Workout timer

The Box Timer is a free and simple workout timer that works just like the timer at your local box. It's perfect for workouts at home, outside or at your box.

Whether you are into functional fitness, weight lifting, stretching of any other type of workouts – The Box Timer will keep track of the time for you!

It could possibly be the best workout timer app there is!

Features

Count up, count down!

Set your workout time cap and press start, the timer will beep when the time is up. Perfect for AMRAP and For Time workouts.

Intervals

Crush any EMOM or Tabata workout with the interval timer! Use it with or without rest time – it's up to you!

Tap to pause

Not all workouts turn out as planned, tap the timer to pause and once more to start again.

No annoying ads

It's just a timer – this is not an app with annoying ads or bloated with useless features.
